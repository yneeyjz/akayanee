Main Navigation

Mobile Version

When on screens that are less than 768px, such as mobile phones and small tablets, the navigation menu is fixed at the top of the screen. The menu will extend once the hamburger icon on the top right corner is selected. This style applies globally to all pages. The following shows an example of the extended version of the navigation.

Desktop Version

On big screens that are equal to or larger than 768px, such as large tablets, laptops, and desktops, the navigation bar is located on the left side of the page. The menu options can be navigated and selected anytime regardless where of the page is scrolled to. This style applies globally to all pages.

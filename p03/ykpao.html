YK Pao School

Working with the YK Pao School foundation department, I created the annual sponsorship booklet to introduce the school's education system and to allow the potential audience to grow interests about the sponsorship program. As a relevant marketing source, this project does not simply deliver information to donors and parents of future students but also mantains school's image to the public.

gain the info

The first step to start the design process is to gather text content from the foundation department. In this document, content are organized into different chapters, therefore it is important to aware the amount of the text before working on the layout.

â€‹To maintain the school's brand identity, I decided to adapt the brand color scheme that has been frequently applied to previous marketing materials. The green symbolizes the elements of growth and youth, yet the variety of tones implies the multi-dimensional principle that the school obtains in their education system.

further development

Challenge I found for this project was the amount of texts was fixed and was expected to fit into certain pages, In addition, certain images were requested to be added onto specific pages. Hence, I sketched various layouts depends on the length of the content and number of the images would be included on the page. Since the content was broke into different sections, each one of them were designed in specific layout to distinguish from other sections.
